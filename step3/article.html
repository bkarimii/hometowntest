Horaman is a small city with an age of several thousand years in Kurdistan of Iran.

The very beautiful nature and its special architecture make this city a very interesting place for tourists.

Cultural Landscape of Hawraman or Cultural Landscape of Uramanat
the 26th tangible cultural heritage of Iran. This remote and mountainous landscape
bears testimony to the traditional culture of the Hawrami people, an agropastoral Kurdish tribe that has inhabited the region since about 3000 BCE.
